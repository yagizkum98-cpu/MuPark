# MuPark Voice-Over Script (60-90 sec)

## Version Info

- Date:
- Version:
- Tone: confident / modern / clear
- Target duration: 75 sec

## Script

[0-08 sec]  
In urban traffic, one of the biggest invisible problems is parking search. MuPark makes this process smart and measurable.

[08-20 sec]  
With a single camera and AI, parking occupancy is monitored in real time. Drivers view available spots in the mobile app and reserve within seconds.

[20-35 sec]  
They navigate directly to the spot and enter with a QR code. Electronic bollard control protects the reserved area from unauthorized use.

[35-50 sec]  
For municipalities and operators, all activity is visible in one live dashboard. Occupancy, no-shows, and revenue impact are tracked in one place.

[50-65 sec]  
In the first phase, MuPark focuses on smart parking reservation, live occupancy visibility, and unified field operations.

[65-75 sec]  
MuPark digitizes parking operations, reduces congestion, and improves urban quality of life.

## Recording Notes

- Recommended speed: 135-145 words per minute
- Emphasis words: MuPark, real-time, reservation, municipality dashboard
- Leave 0.2-0.4 sec breathing gap between key transitions
